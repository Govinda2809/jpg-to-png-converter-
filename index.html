How it works

  1. Choose or drop a JPG image (keeps it local).

  3. Your PNG is instantly downloaded — no uploads or storage.

FAQ

Is my image uploaded anywhere?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser and nothing is sent to a server.

What file types are supported?

Only JPG / JPEG images are accepted. PNG output is generated from your file client-side.

What about very large images?

Very large images will be downscaled to a safe maximum dimension to prevent browser memory errors. This preserves aspect ratio and aims for a balance between quality and reliability.
